## SALARY CONVERSION FORMULA
```
salary_in_local = salary_in_usd × exchange_rate

Example: Software Engineer Senior ($165K USD)
  Canada: $165,000 × 1.36 = C$224,400
  UK: $165,000 × 0.79 = £130,350
  India: $165,000 × 83.50 = ₹1,37,77,500
  UAE: $165,000 × 3.67 = AED 605,550 (TAX-FREE)
  Pakistan: $165,000 × 278 = PKR 45,870,000
  Nigeria: $165,000 × 1,550 = ₦255,750,000
```
