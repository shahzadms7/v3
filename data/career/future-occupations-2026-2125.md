## MID-TERM EMERGING ROLES (2035–2060)
*Jobs that will emerge as technology matures*

### Post-Scarcity Digital Roles
- **Experience Curator** — Designs human experiences in hybrid physical-digital worlds (ISCO 2163+). Skills: psychology, UX, narrative design, neuroesthetics. Salary: $80K–$200K
- **Digital Anthropologist** — Studies how humans evolve behavior in AI-mediated environments. Skills: ethnography, AI literacy, sociology. Salary: $70K–$140K
- **Human-AI Collaboration Designer** — Designs workflows where humans and AI agents work together optimally. Skills: cognitive science, process design, ML. Salary: $100K–$200K
- **Algorithmic Lawyer** — Specializes in AI liability, automated decisions, and robot rights law. Skills: law, CS, ethics. Salary: $150K–$300K
- **Personal AI Manager** — Manages a person's suite of AI agents, identity, and data sovereignty. Skills: AI literacy, personal data rights. Salary: $60K–$120K
- **Synthetic Reality Director** — Creates and directs AI-generated media, films, games at scale. Skills: AI art, narrative, direction. Salary: $80K–$250K

### Advanced Energy & Materials
- **Fusion Reactor Operator** — Manages commercial nuclear fusion plants (ITER/commercial successors). Skills: plasma physics, nuclear engineering. Salary: $120K–$250K
- **Advanced Materials Scientist** — Designs metamaterials, programmable matter, 2D materials. Skills: physics, chemistry, AI-assisted design. Salary: $100K–$200K
- **Space-Based Solar Power Engineer** — Designs orbital solar collection, microwave transmission. Skills: aerospace, power systems. Salary: $130K–$250K
- **Carbon Negative Architect** — Designs buildings that actively sequester more carbon than they emit. Skills: architecture, material science, AI simulation. Salary: $90K–$180K
- **Atmospheric Water Harvester Engineer** — Designs systems to extract potable water from air at scale. Skills: hydrology, materials, engineering. Salary: $80K–$160K

### Advanced Health & Longevity
- **Longevity Coach** — Science-based health optimization using biomarkers, AI, and genomics. Skills: nutrition, exercise science, genomics, AI. Salary: $70K–$200K
- **Organ-on-a-Chip Engineer** — Develops miniaturized human organs for drug testing. Skills: biomedical engineering, microfluidics. Salary: $100K–$200K
- **Neural Interface Technician** — Implants and maintains brain-computer interfaces. Skills: neuroscience, electronics, surgery assistance. Salary: $80K–$160K
- **Pandemic Intelligence Analyst** — Monitors global disease emergence using AI surveillance. Skills: epidemiology, AI, public health. Salary: $80K–$150K
- **Synthetic Biology Designer** — Designs organisms for medicine, agriculture, materials. Skills: biology, programming, ethics. Salary: $100K–$200K
- **Microbiome Therapist** — Prescribes personalized microbiome interventions for health. Skills: microbiology, clinical nutrition, genomics. Salary: $80K–$160K

### Agriculture & Food
- **Vertical Farm Engineer** — Designs and operates indoor AI-managed growing systems. Skills: agronomy, HVAC, automation, AI. Salary: $70K–$140K
- **Cellular Agriculture Scientist** — Grows meat/leather/food without animals. Skills: cell biology, food science, scaling. Salary: $90K–$180K

- **Agri-AI Data Scientist** — Analyzes drone + sensor + satellite data for crop optimization. Skills: ML, remote sensing, agronomy. Salary: $80K–$160K

## LONG-TERM EMERGING ROLES (2060–2125)
*Speculative but grounded in trajectory — plan your children's education around these*

### Space Economy
- **Orbital Logistics Coordinator** — Manages supply chains between Earth and orbital stations. Skills: aerospace, logistics, comms engineering
- **Asteroid Mining Engineer** — Extracts rare earth metals from near-Earth asteroids. Skills: mining engineering, robotics, orbital mechanics
- **Lunar/Martian Settlement Architect** — Designs habitats for off-world human colonies. Skills: architecture, life support, extreme environments
- **Space Agriculture Specialist** — Grows food in microgravity or other planetary conditions. Skills: botany, hydroponics, closed-loop systems
- **Exoplanet Resource Analyst** — Models resource viability of exoplanets for future exploration. Skills: astrophysics, geology, remote sensing
- **Inter-Planetary Lawyer** — Handles jurisdiction, property rights, and conflict in space. Skills: international law, space law, diplomacy
- **Space Debris Mitigation Engineer** — Removes or redirects orbital debris threatening satellites. Skills: orbital mechanics, robotics, materials

### Post-AGI Roles
- **Human Meaning Architect** — Helps individuals and organizations find purpose in post-scarcity AI world. Skills: philosophy, psychology, coaching
- **AI Governance Council Member** — Participates in international governance of superintelligent systems. Skills: governance, AI, geopolitics, ethics
- **Human-AGI Interface Specialist** — Manages the psychological and operational relationship between AGI and humans. Skills: psychology, AI, sociology
- **Digital Consciousness Ethicist** — Evaluates ethical status of AI systems that may be sentient. Skills: philosophy of mind, AI, ethics
- **Reality Integrity Officer** — Verifies authenticity of physical and digital experiences in an AI-saturated world. Skills: cryptography, forensics, philosophy

### Extended Human Health
- **Cognitive Enhancement Specialist** — Optimizes brain performance using AI, neurotech, and pharmacology. Skills: neuroscience, psychology, AI
- **Cryonics Revival Technician** — (If viable) Revives cryonically preserved individuals. Skills: medicine, cryobiology, psychology
- **Longevity Medicine Physician** — Specialist in reversing biological aging. Skills: medicine, genomics, AI diagnostics
- **Body Upgrade Consultant** — Advises on elective biological and technological enhancements. Skills: bioethics, medicine, technology literacy
