## What Recruiters Actually Look For (2026)
1. **First 6 seconds:** Name, current title, current company, years, top 3 skills
2. **Next 20 seconds:** Achievement bullets with NUMBERS, education, certifications
3. **Instant reject triggers:** Typos, generic objective, no dates, walls of text, duty bullets
4. **ATS kill words:** "Responsible for", "Duties included", "Helped with", "Assisted in"
5. **ATS power words:** "Achieved", "Delivered", "Increased by X%", "Reduced X by $Y"

## Visa & Immigration Hidden Truths
### Express Entry (Canada) — What They Don't Tell You
- CRS score of 470+ needed (actual invitations, not minimum)
- Provincial Nominee adds 600 points (almost guaranteed ITA)
- Canadian work experience adds 40-80 points
- LMIA processing: 3-6 months (not 2-4 weeks as advertised)
- French language bonus: 50+ points (even basic level helps)

### H-1B (USA) — Hidden Facts
- 85,000 cap but 780,000+ applications in 2025
- Lottery odds: ~11% first attempt
- L-1 intra-company transfer: NO cap, no lottery
- O-1 extraordinary ability: NO cap, 4-6 month processing
- STEM OPT: 3 years work authorization without H-1B
- Canadian TN visa: No cap, instant at border for 60+ occupations

### UK Skilled Worker — Hidden
- Salary threshold increased to £38,700 (Apr 2024)
- Health & care worker route: lower threshold £29,000
- Graduate route: 2 years post-study work, no sponsor needed
- Scale-up visa: 6 months then free to change employer

### Australia — Hidden
- Points test: 65 minimum but 80+ realistic for invitation
- State sponsorship adds 5-15 points (some states faster)
- Regional visa (491): 3 years then PR, less competition
- Working holiday (417): Ages 18-35, 23 countries eligible
