# Interview Preparation Intelligence 2026
**What gets candidates hired — from first impression to final offer**

## The 90-Second Rule
33% of hiring decisions are made in the first 90 seconds. First impressions include:
- Handshake/greeting confidence
- Eye contact and posture
- Opening statement clarity
- Professional appearance
- Energy and enthusiasm level

## Top 20 Behavioral Interview Questions (STAR Method Required)
1. Tell me about a time you led a team through a difficult project
2. Describe a situation where you had to deal with a difficult colleague
3. Give an example of when you failed and what you learned
4. Tell me about a time you had to make a decision with incomplete information
5. Describe your biggest professional achievement
6. How do you handle tight deadlines and pressure?
7. Tell me about a time you disagreed with your manager
8. Describe a situation where you had to adapt to change quickly
9. Give an example of when you went above and beyond
10. Tell me about a time you had to persuade someone
11. How do you prioritize when everything is urgent?
12. Describe a time you identified and solved a problem proactively
13. Tell me about a project that required learning new skills quickly
14. How do you handle receiving critical feedback?
15. Describe a time you had to manage competing stakeholders
16. Tell me about your experience with cross-functional teams
17. Give an example of data-driven decision making
18. How do you ensure quality in your work?
19. Describe a time you mentored or coached someone
20. Tell me about a time you had to deliver bad news

## Technical Interview Formats by Industry
| Industry | Common Format | Duration | Tips |
|----------|--------------|----------|------|
| Tech/Software | Coding + System Design + Behavioral | 4-6 rounds | Practice LeetCode, system design |
| Finance | Case study + Technical + Fit | 3-4 rounds | Know market trends, mental math |
| Consulting | Case interview + Fit | 2-3 rounds | Framework thinking, structured answers |
| Healthcare | Clinical scenarios + Behavioral | 2-3 rounds | Patient-first language, HIPAA awareness |
| Government | Panel interview + Written test | 1-2 rounds | STAR format mandatory, formal language |

## Questions to Ask the Interviewer (shows preparation)
1. What does success look like in this role in the first 90 days?
2. What are the biggest challenges facing the team right now?
3. How would you describe the team culture?
4. What opportunities for growth and development exist?
5. What's the typical career path for someone in this position?

## Salary Negotiation in Interviews
- Never give a number first — ask for the budgeted range
- Research: Glassdoor, PayScale, Levels.fyi (tech), LinkedIn Salary
- Counter-offer: Ask for 10-20% above their first offer
- Non-salary: Negotiate PTO, remote days, signing bonus, equity, training budget
- Script: "Based on my research and the value I bring, I'm targeting [range]. Is there flexibility?"

## Post-Interview Follow-Up
- Thank-you email: Within 24 hours, personalized per interviewer
- Reference check: Prepare 3 references, brief them on the role
- Follow-up timeline: If no response in 1 week, send polite check-in
- Multiple offers: Honest communication, ask for deadline extension if needed
