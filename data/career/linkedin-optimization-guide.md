# LinkedIn Optimization — What Recruiters Actually Search For
**87% of recruiters use LinkedIn FIRST. Make yourself findable.**

## LINKEDIN HEADLINE FORMULAS (proven formats)
### Formula 1: Title + Specialty + Achievement
`Senior Cloud Engineer | AWS & Azure | Saved $2.3M via cloud migration`

### Formula 2: Title + Industry + Value Prop
`Data Scientist | Healthcare AI | Turning patient data into better outcomes`

### Formula 3: Title + Skills + Open to Work
`Full Stack Developer | React, Python, AWS | Open to Remote Opportunities`

### Formula 4: Student/Career Changer
`Aspiring Data Analyst | Google Certified | Building portfolio at github.com/name`

### Formula 5: Executive
`VP Engineering | Scaling teams 10→100 | $50M+ product launches | AI-First Strategy`

## WHAT RECRUITERS SEARCH FOR (exact keywords)
### Top searched terms by recruiters (2026 data)
1. Job title (exact match): "Software Engineer", "Data Analyst"
2. Skills: "Python", "AWS", "Kubernetes", "React"
3. Certifications: "PMP", "AWS Certified", "CPA"
4. Company names: "Google", "McKinsey", "JPMorgan"
5. Location: "Toronto", "Remote", "San Francisco"
6. Education: "MBA", "Computer Science", "Stanford"

### Where keywords MUST appear (ranked by LinkedIn search weight)
| Location | Search Weight | What to Put |
|----------|--------------|-------------|
| Headline | Highest | Title + top 2 skills + achievement |
| Job titles | Very high | Exact role name (not creative titles) |
| Skills section | High | All relevant skills (max 50) |
| About/Summary | Medium | Keywords woven into narrative |
| Experience bullets | Medium | Achievements with keywords |
| Education | Low | Degree, school, relevant courses |

## PROFILE PHOTO RULES
- Professional headshot or high-quality selfie
- Face takes up 60% of frame
- Plain or blurred background
- Good lighting (face towards window)
- Smile — profiles with smiles get 14x more views
- NO: sunglasses, group photos, cropped party pics, logos

## LINKEDIN ACTIVITY (what makes you visible)
| Action | Frequency | Impact |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Post original content | 2-3x/week | Highest visibility |
| Comment on others' posts | Daily | Builds network |
| Share industry articles | 1-2x/week | Shows awareness |
| Engage with target companies | 3-5x/week | Gets noticed |
| Update profile | Monthly | Stays fresh in search |
| Publish long article | 1-2x/month | Thought leadership |

## COMMON LINKEDIN MISTAKES
| Mistake | Impact | Fix |
|---------|--------|-----|
| "Open to Work" green banner | Looks desperate to some recruiters | Use private "Open to Work" setting instead |
| Generic headline ("Seeking Opportunities") | Invisible in search | Use formula above with keywords |
| No profile photo | 21x fewer views | Add professional photo |
| Empty summary | Missed keyword opportunity | Use template above |
| Listing duties not achievements | Boring, forgettable | Every bullet needs a NUMBER |
| 500+ connections but zero engagement | Dead profile | Comment on 3 posts daily |
| Not customizing connection requests | Low acceptance rate | Always add personalized note |
