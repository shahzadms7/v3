## Overview

The International Standard Classification of Occupations (ISCO-08) organizes all jobs into **10 Major Groups**, subdivided into sub-major, minor, and unit groups. This database covers **150+ key occupations** across all 10 major groups with salary data, demand trends, AI risk, skills, certifications, and hiring hotspots for 2026.

### Legend
| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Growing** | 5%+ job growth projected through 2030 |
| **Stable** | -2% to +5% job growth |
| **Declining** | Negative growth or significant automation displacement |
| **Low** | < 20% tasks automatable in next decade |
| **Medium** | 20-50% tasks automatable |
| **High** | > 50% tasks automatable |
