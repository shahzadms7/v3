## SKILLS THAT AI CANNOT REPLACE (invest here)
- Physical trades (plumbing, electrical, welding) — AI has no hands
- Healthcare bedside care — empathy cannot be automated
- Complex negotiation — requires reading humans
- Creative direction — AI generates, humans curate
- Crisis leadership — panic requires human judgment
- Ethical decision-making — accountability requires humans
- Cultural sensitivity — context AI doesn't understand
- Skilled construction/maintenance — physical world
