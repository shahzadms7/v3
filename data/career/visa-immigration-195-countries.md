## HIDDEN IMMIGRATION TRICKS (what agents won't tell you)

### Express Entry (Canada) — Hidden Points
- Get IELTS 8+ in each band = 124 points (vs 110 for 7+)
- French TEF B2 = 50+ bonus points (even basic French helps)
- Provincial Nominee = AUTO +600 points (almost guaranteed invite)
- Canadian education = 15-30 bonus points
- Sibling in Canada = 15 points
- Job offer with LMIA = 50-200 points (but LMIA is expensive/slow)

### H-1B (USA) — Hidden Alternatives
- L-1 (Intra-company) — NO cap, no lottery. Work at US subsidiary.
- O-1 (Extraordinary) — NO cap. 10+ years experience can qualify.
- TN (Canadian/Mexican NAFTA) — NO cap, approved at border same day.
- E-2 (Treaty Investor) — $100K+ investment, manage your own business.
- EB-1 (Green Card directly) — Skip H-1B entirely if extraordinary.
- STEM OPT — 3 years work after US degree, no H-1B needed.

### UK — Hidden Pathways
- Graduate Route — 2 years work after any UK degree, no sponsor
- Global Talent — No sponsor, no salary minimum. For proven leaders.
- Youth Mobility (if eligible country) — 2 years, ages 18-30
- Ancestry Visa — If grandparent born in UK = 5 years unlimited work

### Australia — Hidden
- 491 Regional — Lower points needed, 3 years then PR
- GTI (Global Talent) — 10 categories, fast track, $167K+ salary
- Working Holiday → Employer Sponsorship — Do 2 years, then employer sponsors
